Who We Are

A Last-Days Ministry, Sent to the Nations

Elshaddai Fire Ministries International (EFMI) is a Pentecostal organization devoted to building God's everlasting kingdom. We strive to spread God's word and love to everyone, both online and offline — believing we live in the last days, when the harvest is ready and the call to bring souls into eternal life is most urgent.

Established on January 2, 2020, our ministry has grown into several branches, each carrying the Gospel to a different field: Rising Sparkz for children, Warriors of King Almighty for youth, the United Christian Gospel Group reaching believers on Instagram and WhatsApp, Yeshua Liron Studio for music production, and The Living Verse, our Christian bible-verse and bookstore.

Our commitment remains the same across every branch: to serve the poor, foster a community of believers, and share the transformative power of the Gospel worldwide.

President & Founder

Asbin Jerome B.A., M.Div.

"But by the grace of God I am what I am." — 1 Corinthians 15:10

Born in Trichy into a pastor's family, Asbin Jerome loved serving God from childhood. Jesus Christ called him into ministry in 2016, at age 13, and he was soon filled with the Holy Spirit. He took immersion baptism in 2017.

Through prayer and God's mercy, he was delivered, restored, and called back into ministry. On January 2, 2020, God gave him Elshaddai Fire Ministries International. Today, by His grace, he shares the Gospel with people from many nations. All glory belongs to Jesus Christ, who saved and continues to use him for His Kingdom.

Vice President

Osborn Matthew M.E., M.Div.

Born and brought up in Trichy in a pastor's family, Osborn Matthew was introduced to Christ from childhood and grew up in a Christian environment. After finishing school, he had planned to focus on a career — but God had a different plan.

Through a season of challenges and transition, God redirected his path and called him into pastoral ministry in 2020, with a clear vision to bring young people back to Christ. Working alongside his elder brother, he helped establish EFMI Ministries — a ministry devoted to harvesting souls for eternal life.

Our Story

From a Small Club to a Worldwide Ministry

A journey of faith, trial, revival, and divine purpose.

1
2013

The Humble Beginning

Founded by Bro. Asbin Jerome, Osborn Matthew, and two friends as the Poor Family Help Club (PFHC). The mission was simple and compassionate — providing money, food, and essential items to poor families in need.

2
Divine Call

A Revelation from God

During prayer, Bro. Asbin Jerome received a revelation from Jesus Christ — a call to help people not just in body, but through the Gospel itself. The Lord made clear that spreading His Word would secure eternal life in heaven and save souls from eternal suffering.

3
Growth

Years of Flourishing

For several years the ministry grew and touched many lives across communities. The work expanded, hearts were changed, and the seeds of the Gospel began bearing fruit in ways that went far beyond what the founders had imagined.

4
Trial

A Season of Adversity

A period of deep hardship brought the work to a standstill, leaving the team sorrowful and tested. Yet in that difficult season, Jesus spoke again to Bro. Asbin Jerome — reaffirming His faithfulness and promising that a greater purpose still lay ahead.

5
Jan 2, 2020

EFMI Is Born

With renewed faith and divine guidance, Elshaddai Fire Ministries International was officially established — a more advanced and powerful expression of the same calling. Five branches now carry the Gospel to children, youth, believers worldwide, through music, and through Scripture.

✦
Today

Our Ongoing Commitment

EFMI remains devoted to serving the poor, sharing the message of hope and salvation, and harvesting souls for eternal life. Our journey is one of faith, dedication, and unwavering belief in the transformative power of the Gospel — until He comes.
